Hackathon Help Desk

The podcast on hackathons where caffeine and creativity collide.

Welcome to HackathonParty's AI podcast! A podcast dedicated to the exhilarating, exhausting, and often hilarious world of hackathons. If you've ever pulled an all-nighter trying to bring a crazy idea to life, or if you're just curious about what really goes on at these coding marathons, this podcast is for you. From tales of hacking gone wrong (and right) to practical advice on hacking your way to the top, we'll cover everything you need to know to level up your life with hackathons using our founders Hackathon Survival Guide as a base. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a complete newbie, join us as we explore the world of hackathons, one crazy idea at a time.
